# Splat Explorer

## Tagline

Make your splats look less good (and very great) then send to your friends.

## Description (3 sentences)

Splat Explorer is a browser playground for tweaking how gaussian splats look. Load a `.ply` file (drop your own, or pick a demo) and play with dozens of sliders covering splat shape, screen-space sizing, color quantization, and edge-detection outlines. When you find a look, share it: every slider state encodes to a short URL.

## Origin Story

I love gaussian splats. They are like photogrammetry got hard to understand and easy to render, and I have wondered right from my first introduction what different treatment of the splats themselves would yield visually. This is the tool that lets me (and you) explore that.
